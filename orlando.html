Orlando

Enjoy Your Stay in Orlando

Set foot on an unforgettable journey to Orlando, where endless fun and excitement await at every turn. Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of world-renowned theme parks like Walt Disney World and Universal Studios, where magical experiences and thrilling adventures come to life. Beyond the parks, discover the charm of Orlando's local attractions, such as the Orlando Science Center, providing captivating insights into science and technology, and the serene beauty of Lake Eola Park, offering a peaceful escape into nature. Delve into the heart of the city's rich cultural and arts scene, with venues like the Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts and the Orlando Museum of Art, showcasing the vibrant creativity and talent that define Orlando. Whether you're seeking the adrenaline rush of roller coasters, the wonders of science, the tranquility of nature, or the cultural vibrancy of the arts, Orlando is your gateway to a vacation filled with joy, diversity, and unforgettable memories.

Theme Parks

Orlando, Florida, stands as the ultimate destination for enchanting and action-packed adventures with its world-renowned theme parks—Walt Disney World and Universal Studios. Walt Disney World, a magical realm in itself, encompasses four enchanting parks—Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Disney's Hollywood Studios, and Disney's Animal Kingdom. Each park offers a unique and immersive experience, from meeting beloved Disney characters to embarking on thrilling rides and exploring captivating lands. Meanwhile, Universal Studios Orlando invites visitors to step into the movies with its dynamic duo of theme parks—Universal Studios Florida and Universal's Islands of Adventure. From the spellbinding Wizarding World of Harry Potter to pulse-pounding rides and live entertainment, Universal Studios captivates with its cinematic flair. Together, these iconic parks make Orlando a mecca for family fun and unforgettable memories.

Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is a magical destination that captivates visitors of all ages. Spanning over 25,000 acres, this iconic resort is home to four theme parks—Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Disney's Hollywood Studios, and Disney's Animal Kingdom—each offering a unique and immersive experience. From Cinderella's Castle in Magic Kingdom to the technological wonders of EPCOT, every corner of Walt Disney World is infused with the spirit of imagination and wonder. Beyond the attractions, the resort features diverse dining options, world-class entertainment, and enchanting accommodations. Whether you're meeting beloved Disney characters, enjoying thrilling rides, or savoring delectable treats, Walt Disney World is a place where dreams come to life, creating cherished memories that last a lifetime.

Universal Studios

Universal Studios Orlando is a thrilling and immersive theme park that transports visitors into the heart of their favorite movies and TV shows. Divided into two main parks, Universal Studios Florida and Universal's Islands of Adventure, the resort offers an unparalleled entertainment experience. From the pulse-pounding rides and attractions at Islands of Adventure, including The Wizarding World of Harry Potter, to the movie magic and behind-the-scenes excitement at Universal Studios Florida, every corner of the parks is designed to captivate and delight. Guests can embark on epic adventures, encounter iconic characters, and enjoy live shows that bring their favorite stories to life. With an array of dining, shopping, and entertainment options at Universal CityWalk, Universal Studios Orlando promises an unforgettable, cinematic escape for visitors of all ages.

Local Attractions

Exploring Orlando's rich tapestry of local attractions unveils hidden gems like the Orlando Science Center and the picturesque Lake Eola Park. The Orlando Science Center, a hub of scientific discovery and interactive learning, invites visitors of all ages to delve into the wonders of science through engaging exhibits, live demonstrations, and hands-on activities. It's a place where curiosity knows no bounds, making it an ideal destination for families and science enthusiasts alike. Meanwhile, nestled in the heart of downtown Orlando, Lake Eola Park provides a serene oasis in the midst of the city's vibrant energy. With its iconic swan boats, charming walking paths, and scenic beauty, Lake Eola Park offers a tranquil escape. Visitors can enjoy leisurely strolls, swan boat rides, and even catch live performances at the Walt Disney Amphitheater. Together, these attractions contribute to Orlando's diverse allure, offering a blend of educational exploration and natural serenity.

Orlando Science Center

Immerse yourself in the wonders of discovery at the Orlando Science Center. This dynamic science museum is a beacon of exploration and education. The Orlando Science Center invites visitors of all ages to engage with hands-on exhibits, interactive displays, and captivating demonstrations. From the marvels of outer space to the intricacies of the human body, the center offers a diverse range of experiences that make science come alive. Whether you're a curious child or a lifelong learner, the Orlando Science Center sparks curiosity and fosters a love for the fascinating world of science, making it a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists alike.

Lake Eola Park

Nestled in the heart of downtown Orlando, Lake Eola Park stands as an oasis of tranquility and natural beauty. This iconic park, centered around a picturesque lake, offers a serene escape amid the bustling urban landscape. Visitors can leisurely stroll along the scenic pathways that wind around Lake Eola, taking in breathtaking views of the swan-filled waters and lush greenery. The park is a popular spot for outdoor enthusiasts, offering paddleboat rentals for a charming excursion on the lake. With its vibrant flower beds, charming swan boats, and a signature amphitheater hosting various events, Lake Eola Park is not only a peaceful retreat but also a vibrant hub of community life. It's a perfect destination for picnics, family outings, or simply unwinding in a setting that seamlessly blends nature and urban charm.

Cultural and Arts Scene

Orlando's cultural and arts scene flourishes with a vibrant tapestry of creativity, featuring prominent institutions such as the Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts and the Orlando Museum of Art. The Dr. Phillips Center stands as a beacon for the performing arts, hosting world-class shows, Broadway productions, and captivating performances that elevate Orlando's cultural landscape. This state-of-the-art venue is a testament to the city's commitment to fostering artistic expression. On the visual arts front, the Orlando Museum of Art is a cultural gem, showcasing a diverse collection of contemporary and classical works. Nestled in Loch Haven Park, the museum provides a space for artistic exploration and expression, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in thought-provoking exhibitions and educational programs. Orlando's commitment to fostering creativity and artistic excellence shines through these cultural landmarks, making it a destination where the arts come to life with passion and purpose.

Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts

The Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts stands as a beacon of artistic expression, hosting world-class performances that span from Broadway shows to symphony concerts. This state-of-the-art venue not only captivates audiences with its stellar productions but also serves as a cultural hub that fosters a deep appreciation for the performing arts.
